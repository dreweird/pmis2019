About PMIS

PMIS is a sytematic approach for gathering data or information regarding the Budget Execution Demands (BEDs) which contain the agency plans, spending schedules and physical targets for evaluation and consolidation. It is a tool in keeping up-dated records on the present accomplishment status of financial, physical and disbursement of the department/agency/OUs. This data/records is then processed, integrated, and stored in the centralized database and made available to all who have authority to access it, in a form that suits the department's purpose. This will be the basis for measuring the agency's performance.
